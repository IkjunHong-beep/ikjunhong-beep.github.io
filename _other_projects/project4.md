### **Challenge**- Conventional displays rely on physical media (screens or scattering layers) to form images. Achieving visible image formation directly in free space without a medium remains a significant challenge.

### **Approach**- Investigate acousto-optic interactions in air to induce localized refractive index perturbations. Explore the feasibility of dynamically modulating light in free space for volumetric or mid-air display generation.

### **Techniques**- Acousto-optics, wave propagation in air, Optical modulation and beam shaping  

### **Instruments**- Laser sources and beam-shaping optics, Acoustic transducers / RF drivers, Optical detection systems (cameras, photodetectors)
